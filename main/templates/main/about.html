About
What is this?
Open Cult is a place for people with common interests to meet IRL.
Really though?
It's a meetup.com clone in the spirit of HN. Crazy fast and ridiculously minimal.
Why?
Because meetup.com is slow, has a new dislikable design and does not send emails consistently.
Pricing
For the time being the service is free of charge.
Source
Open Cult is open source. You can find the source code repository on GitHub.
